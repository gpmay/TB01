Call for Solutions Competition Goals

  • Bring attention to solutions that can have impact; understand their benefits and tradeoffs; create public demand for action; and, market demand for results.
  • Inspire and stimulate ambitious solutions and foster collaboration (as well as competition to market). Stimulate cross-fertilization among new and mature solutions and across industry, system and sector boundaries.
  • Create ecosystems of solutions that enable green technology to take off in a way that has impact on global systems such as climate and ocean vitality.
  • Reward exemplary solutions/technology that embody the vision of creating resiliency and planetary sustainability

 

Who we invite:

We invite inventors from established businesses, entrepreneurial ventures, research institutions and labs to showcase solutions that can scale to have a positive impact for sustainability at a planetary level

 

What we are looking for:

Solutions, technology, policy, processes or a combination of both, that:

  • Exhibit multiple benefits
  • Have systemic and game-changing impact
  • Address both mitigation and adaptation to climate change
  • Generate more good & drive less bad
  • Are scalable and adaptable to a broad range of conditions
  • Are feasible and using either current or new technology, resources and know-how
  • Can withstand empirical testing
